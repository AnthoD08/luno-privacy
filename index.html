This privacy policy explains what data Luno collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with. Luno is a bedtime story app for children aged 3 to 10, intended to be set up and used with adult supervision. We have designed Luno so that as little personal data as possible leaves your device.

1. Information we collect

Luno is designed to operate without an account. We do not ask for an email address, a phone number, or a login. The data Luno handles falls into the following categories:

Profile data (entered by you)

  • Child profile name — a first name or nickname you choose, used to personalize the story.
  • Child profile age — a number between 3 and 10, used to adapt vocabulary and themes.

This data is stored only on the device and is sent to our story-generation provider only at the moment a new story is requested (see Third-party services below).

Story preferences and generated content

  • Selected themes, story duration, narrator voice.
  • Stories saved to your library.
  • Bedtime reminder time, theme, night mode setting.

Subscription state

  • Whether you have an active premium subscription, the plan, and renewal status.
  • This is managed by Apple via the App Store. We never see your Apple ID, payment method, or transaction history.

What we do not collect

  • No email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information.
  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access.
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA), no behavioral tracking, no analytics SDKs.
  • No third-party advertising of any kind.

2. How we use it

  • To generate bedtime stories personalized for the child profile you set up.
  • To synthesize narration in the voice you select.
  • To remember your library and preferences so the app feels familiar each evening.
  • To deliver the bedtime reminder you schedule (local notifications only — no remote push).
  • To unlock premium features for users with an active subscription.

3. Third-party services

Luno relies on three third-party services to function. We send each service only the minimum data it needs.

Service What we send Why
Anthropic (Claude API) Child profile first name, age, story themes, lesson, duration. To generate the text of each bedtime story.
ElevenLabs (Text-to-Speech API) Story text, selected voice ID. To synthesize narration audio.
RevenueCat / Apple App Store Anonymous subscription identifiers managed by Apple. To validate your subscription and unlock premium features.

Each service operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • ElevenLabs Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other party.

4. Where data is stored

  • On your device: profiles, library, preferences, narrator selection, bedtime schedule, theme, and subscription cache all live in on-device storage. They are never sent to a Luno-operated server (we don't operate one).
  • In transit to third-party services: only at the moment a story is generated or narrated. The information needed for that one request is sent over HTTPS and is then governed by the third party's policy listed above.

5. Data about children

Luno is intended to be set up by a parent or guardian and used with adult supervision. While Luno is not submitted to the Apple Kids Category, much of its content is directed at children:

  • The only data tied to a child is the first name and age you choose to enter. If you prefer the child's real first name not to leave the device, use a nickname — story generation works the same way.
  • We collect no behavioral data, no contact data, no precise location, and no advertising identifiers from anyone, including children.
  • You can delete a child profile at any time from the Settings tab. Deletion removes the name and age from the device.
  • Premium subscriptions can only be purchased from inside the app, and Apple requires the device account holder to authenticate every purchase.

6. Retention and deletion

  • Profile and library data is kept on the device until you delete it through the app or uninstall Luno. Uninstalling permanently removes all on-device data.
  • Story-generation requests sent to Anthropic and ElevenLabs may be retained by those providers per their own retention schedules — please consult their policies linked above.
  • Subscription history is retained by Apple per Apple's policies; we have no access to your Apple ID or account.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data (including under the GDPR in the EU/UK and the CCPA in California). Because Luno keeps your data on the device:

  • Access — open the Settings tab to see every profile, preference, and saved story.
  • Correction — tap a profile to change the name or age, or use the pickers to change voice and theme.
  • Deletion — delete a profile from Settings, or uninstall the app to remove all data.
  • Subscription — managed entirely from your Apple ID: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.

For requests concerning data held by Anthropic or ElevenLabs in connection with a story request, contact those providers directly using the policies linked above.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change how Luno handles data, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notice on the next launch.
